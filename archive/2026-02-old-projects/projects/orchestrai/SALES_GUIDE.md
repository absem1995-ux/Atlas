## Discovery Questions

When talking to prospects, ask:

1. **Problem Discovery:**
   - "What's your current cost of [function]?" (CS, admin, marketing)
   - "How long does it take to [task]?"
   - "What's the biggest bottleneck?"

2. **Solution Fit:**
   - "Would it help if you could automate [repetitive task]?"
   - "What would you do with 15 extra hours/week?"
   - "What's stopping you from [scaling/hiring]?"

3. **Timeline:**
   - "When do you need this solved?"
   - "What does success look like in 30 days?"

4. **Budget:**
   - "What's your rough budget for this?"
   - "How much are you currently spending on this function?"
