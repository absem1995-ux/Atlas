## Brand Identity

"Deploy intelligent AI agents for your business in hours. Customer service, administrative tasks, marketing—your choice. We handle setup, integration, and ongoing management."

### Value Proposition
- **For Businesses:** Automate repetitive work, reduce costs, scale faster
- **For Managers:** Focus on strategy, not admin work

### Market Positioning
- **NOT** a generic "AI chatbot" company

- **IS** for businesses that need specific agents deployed quickly

- Team size: 3-20 people

## Business Model

### Revenue Streams

**1. Setup Fee (One-time)**
- Customer Service Agent: $1,500-3,000
- Virtual Assistant Agent: $2,000-4,000
- Marketing Agent: $1,000-2,500

*Includes: system setup, integrations, training, initial configuration*

**2. Monthly Recurring Revenue**
- Base Agent License: $500-1,000/month
- Includes: hosting, updates, basic support

**3. Add-ons & Premium Support**
- Advanced integrations: +$200-500/month
- Custom training/fine-tuning: +$300-1,000
- Priority support (24h response): +$200/month
- White-label/private deployment: +$500/month

**4. Success-Based Pricing (Optional)**
- "Performance tier": Pay 15-25% of savings we generate
- E.g., Customer service agent saves $2K/month → we get $300-500

### Projected Unit Economics

**Customer Service Agent:**
- Setup fee: $2,000 (one-time, takes 4 hours)
- Monthly fee: $700
- Expected customer lifetime: 24 months
- Total revenue per customer: $2,000 + ($700 × 24) = $18,800
- Your cost (mostly infrastructure): ~30% = $5,640
- Net margin: $13,160 (70%)

**Virtual Assistant Agent:**
- Setup fee: $3,000 (one-time, takes 6 hours)
- Monthly fee: $900
- Expected customer lifetime: 24 months
- Total revenue per customer: $3,000 + ($900 × 24) = $24,600
- Your cost: ~35% = $8,610
- Net margin: $15,990 (65%)

**Marketing Agent:**
- Setup fee: $1,500 (one-time, takes 3 hours)
- Monthly fee: $800
- Expected customer lifetime: 18 months (higher churn)
- Total revenue per customer: $1,500 + ($800 × 18) = $15,900
- Your cost: ~25% = $3,975
- Net margin: $11,925 (75%)

### Break-Even Analysis

**Monthly overhead:**
- Infrastructure (Firebase, servers): $200
- Stripe/payment processor fees: ~5% of revenue
- Email/communication: $50
- Total fixed: ~$250 + variable

**To break even (first year):**
- Need 3-5 paying customers at average monthly fee
- Revenue: $2,500-4,500/month
- Gross margin: $1,750-3,150/month
- Break-even: ~Month 2-3 if you land customers

### Phase 1: Launch & Authority Building (Month 1)
- Launch website (landing page + blog)

- Deploy agents for 5-10 pilot customers

- Goal: 10-15 paying customers

### Phase 3: Scale (Month 4+)
- Productize based on feedback
- Hire implementation team (if needed)
- Run paid ads (Google, LinkedIn)
- Partner with agencies/consultants
- Goal: 50+ customers

## Competitive Landscape

### Direct Competitors
- **Make** (automation platform) - general tool, not specific agents
- **Zapier** (automation) - too broad, requires configuration
- **HubSpot** (CRM with automation) - enterprise focused
- **Intercom** (customer communication) - focused on chat only

### Why OrchestrAI Wins
- ✅ **Pre-configured agents** (not DIY tools)
- ✅ **Specific use cases** (customer service, VA, marketing)
- ✅ **White-glove setup** (we do integration, not you)
- ✅ **Managed service** (ongoing support, optimization)
- ✅ **Pricing** ($500-1K/month vs. $100-300K/year enterprise)

### Differentiation
- Not a platform (complex, requires skill)
- Not a consulting firm (expensive, slow)
- **Is a managed service** (simple, affordable, fast)
