# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** February 16, 2026

---

## Introduction

FastTrack ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") operates the FastTrack mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of your information through the App.

**Key Principle:** Your privacy is important to us. We collect minimal data, use it only for the App's functionality, and never sell your information to third parties.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

**Account Information:**
- Email address
- Password (hashed, we never store plain passwords)
- Optional: Goal (weight loss, health, clarity)
- Optional: Goal weight, current weight

**Fasting Data:**
- Fasting sessions (date, time, protocol, duration)
- Weight logs (date, weight, optional notes)

**Notification Preferences:**
- Notification enabled/disabled status
- Preferred notification time

**How long we keep it:** As long as your account exists. You can delete your account anytime in Settings → Account → Delete Account.

### 1.2 Information We Don't Collect

We do NOT collect:
- ❌ Your real name
- ❌ Your location
- ❌ Your device ID or IDFA
- ❌ Browser cookies
- ❌ Third-party app data

### 1.3 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the App, we automatically collect:
- **Device type and OS:** iPhone, iOS version (for compatibility)
- **App usage:** Features used, errors encountered (for improvement)
- **Timestamps:** When you use the app

**We do NOT:**
- Track your location
- Access your photos
- Access your contacts
- Access your calendar
- Sell this data to advertisers

## 2. How We Use Your Information

### 2.1 To Provide the App
- Display your fasting streaks and stats
- Generate weekly PDF reports

### 2.2 To Improve the App
- Analyze crash reports and errors

### 2.3 To Communicate
- Send you reminders (only if you enabled them)
- Respond to support emails
- Announce app updates

### 2.4 We Do NOT Use Your Data For
- ❌ Advertising or marketing
- ❌ Selling to third parties
- ❌ Behavioral profiling
- ❌ Discrimination or targeting

## 3. Data Storage & Security

### 3.1 Where Your Data Lives
- **Authentication:** Google Firebase (encrypted, industry-standard)
- **App Data:** Google Firebase Realtime Database (encrypted in transit and at rest)
- **Backups:** Distributed across Google Cloud data centers

### 3.2 Security Measures
- ✅ HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
- ✅ BCRYPT hashing for passwords
- ✅ Firebase security rules (only you can read your data)
- ✅ No plain-text passwords stored
- ✅ Automatic security updates

### 3.3 Limitations
While we use industry-standard security, no system is 100% secure. We recommend:
- Use a strong password
- Keep your device updated
- Don't share your login credentials

## 4. Data Sharing

### 4.1 We Share Data With
**Google Firebase:**
- Your email and hashed password (for authentication)
- Your fasting and weight data (stored on their servers)
- **Why:** To sync across your devices and provide cloud backup
- **Their privacy:** [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

**Service Providers:**
- Analytics provider (to track app crashes and errors)
- Email service (if you request support)
- Payment processor (Stripe, for premium subscriptions)

### 4.2 We Do NOT Share Data With
- ❌ Advertisers or marketing companies
- ❌ Data brokers
- ❌ Social media platforms
- ❌ Health insurance companies
- ❌ Government agencies (without legal request)

### 4.3 Legal Requests
If required by law (court order, subpoena, government request), we may disclose your data. We will:
- Inform you if legally possible
- Only disclose what's legally required
- Cooperate with law enforcement

## 5. Your Rights

### 5.1 Access
You can access all your data anytime by:
1. Opening the App
2. Going to Settings → Account
3. Seeing your email, goal, and all logged fasts/weights

### 5.2 Correction
You can update your data anytime:
- Edit your goal in Settings
- Re-log your weight with new value
- Update notification time

### 5.3 Deletion
You can delete your account anytime:
1. Settings → Account → Delete Account
2. Confirm deletion (cannot be undone)
3. All your data is permanently removed from our servers

### 5.4 Portability
You can export your data:
1. iOS: Open Files app
2. Find FastTrack folder
3. Export logs as JSON

### 5.5 Objection
You can:
- Disable push notifications (Settings → Notifications)
- Stop using the app anytime
- Opt out of analytics (if feature is available)
