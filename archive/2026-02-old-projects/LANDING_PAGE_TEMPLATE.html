Frequently Asked Questions

What is intermittent fasting?

Intermittent fasting is an eating pattern that cycles between periods of fasting and eating. 16:8 means fasting for 16 hours and eating during an 8-hour window.

Do I need the premium version?

No. The free version includes the full timer, streak tracking, and weight logging. Premium adds weekly reports and advanced notifications.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Premium is a subscription that you can cancel anytime in your iOS Settings → Subscriptions.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your fasting and weight data is encrypted and stored securely. We never sell your data. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Sign up with email, choose your fasting protocol, start tracking. It takes 2 minutes to get started.
