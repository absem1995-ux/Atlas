## 2. Description

```
Track your intermittent fasting streaks and reach your health goals.

FastTrack makes fasting simple. Start a timer, build your streak, 
and watch your progress with real-time stats.

FEATURES
• Simple timer for 16:8, 18:6, 20:4, OMAD, or custom fasting protocols
• Streak counter to stay motivated (current + longest)
• Weight tracking with daily logs
• Smart dashboard with key stats (consistency %, avg duration, weight change)
• Weekly PDF reports (Premium)
• Push notifications with smart timing (Premium)
• Apple Watch support for quick control
• Works offline - full functionality without internet

WHAT USERS LOVE
✓ No ads or spam
✓ Privacy first - your data stays yours
✓ Minimal, focused design
✓ Free forever (or upgrade to Premium for reports)

PRIVACY
Your fasting data is 100% private. We never sell your data to advertisers 
or third parties. Learn more: fasttrack.app/privacy

QUESTIONS?

Start your fasting journey today. 📱
```
